Privacy Policy — Tic Tac Toe

Last updated: 2nd July 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Tic Tac Toe mobile application ("the app") named "Unlimited XOXO: Tic Tac Toe", published by Whiskay ("we", "us", "our") on the Google Play Store.

Information We Collect

The app is an offline game. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app does not require an account and does not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or files. No gameplay data is transmitted to our servers.

Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device only, and are removed when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify your device, such as advertising identifiers, IP address, and crash logs. This data is collected and processed by those providers under their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services

You can reset or limit your advertising identifier at any time in your device settings.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because we do not collect personal data, there is nothing for us to retain or delete. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, email us at contact@whiskay.dev.
